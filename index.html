COPA-leaderboard
A standardized benchmark for certified robustness of RL algorithms

The goal of COPA-leaderboard is to systematically certify the robustness of different offline RL algorithms based on certification criteria such as per-state action and the lower bound of cumulative reward. The related paper can be found here.
